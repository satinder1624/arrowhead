Diplomatic Security

GET FREE INQUIRY

Diplomatic security: Everyone and everywhere has to be secure. But the level of security required to be protected is different in all cases. Diplomatic security is something which puts a high level of responsibility on guard's shoulders. For that these guards are highly trained, certified to be diplomatic security guards and take their job very very seriously.

Why diplomatic guards are special?

  • These guards have hawk-eyes which they develop during the intense training.
  • They have special training in addition to security guard. They require specific training and to clear additional tests to be appointed as diplomatic guards.
  • The way they are trained, prepared and paid, they definitely understand the sincerity their job requires, thus can be trusted to shield the important personalities and assets.
