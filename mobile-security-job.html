Mobile Security Guard

Description

Observes the activities at the site and makes daily reports, patrols the site frequently for safety and immediately takes action and reports any suspicious activity observed. Controls the access to the building and provides the access to the staff and clients, manages the crowd during emergency situations and evacuates the site, follows the protocols for emergency situations. Additional relevant duties may be assigned as per the requirement of the client.

Requirements

  • High school diploma or equivalent
  • Valid security guard licence
  • Minimum 6 months of experience as a security guard in Canada
  • Eligible to work in Canada
